Keyword Mapping for the ICL Open Access Book Series

As of December 2024, the Open Access book series has published five volumes, featuring over 130 articles. These articles include approximately 500 distinct keywords. To provide an overview and assist in article searches, these keywords have been categorized into 14 groups, and a tree map has been created. The tree map allows users to: • View the keywords organized by category. • Access individual articles where specific keywords are used by navigating through the map. This tool enhances the usability of the series, enabling efficient exploration and a better understanding of the scope of the research.
